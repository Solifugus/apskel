* The following describes the purpose of each directory (or directory type) that
should exist under the apskel/ directory:

- apskel/notes.txt
- apskel/README.dm
- apskel/LICENSE
- apskel/webroot/
- apskel/identification.php
- apskel/environments_{version}/
- apskel/application_{version}/
- apskel/tools/
